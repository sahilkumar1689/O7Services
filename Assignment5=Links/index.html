CCC-Course on Computer Concept


What is CCC course?


NIELIT course is designed to impact a basic level of IT Literacy.After completing the NIELIT course,aspirant can use the computer for the basic purpose of preparing their personal/business letter,viewing information on the internet,receiving and sending main, etc.


What is the qualification for CCC?


There is no minimum qualification required to apply for or take the course on computer concept examination.


Whar is included in CCC courses?

NIELIT CCC course cover the basic concepts related to computer hardware and software,presentation,speedsheets,internet and email.

CCC-Course on Computer Concept


What is CCA Certificate?


CCA, an acronym for Certificate in computer Application,is a vocational certificate program that aims to upgrade an individual's computer skills,enhancing their employbility.


What is the qualification for CCC?


Candidate must have passed graduation or equivalent level (with Mathmatics as a subject of study at 10+2 or graduation level) examination from a recognized university.He/she must have obtained at least 50% (relaxable for reserved category cadidates) of the marks at graduation or equivalent level.


What subject in CCC?
Certificate in Computer Application is a certificate course to study computer Application in depth.The course imparts theoretical,practical and knowledge to its learners about various computers tools that are used in day to day life.
